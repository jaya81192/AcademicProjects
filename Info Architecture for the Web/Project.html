The Indiana University School of Public Health along with the Indiana Minority Health Coalition is exploring patient and provider experiences of linkage to and retention in HIV care in Indiana. We are trying to gather the insights of those with relevant experience working in the area of HIV care and services.

Our hope is to develop a set of strong recommendations that will contribute to improving linkage to and retention of patients in care. We welcome you to learn more about these efforts and welcome you to participate in our study or attend one of the community events. Please contact us if you have any questions or would like more information.



This project is funded through Indiana University School of Public Health and Intends to engage and collaborate with individuals living with HIV and/or friends and family members to listen, learn and empower individuals to inform and improve HIV care.
